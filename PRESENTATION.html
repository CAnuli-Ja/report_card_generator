📝 How to Use (Step 1-2)

Step 1: Upload Excel File

Click "Choose Excel File" and select your spreadsheet with student data

Note: First column should have student numbers

Step 2: Select Sheet

Choose the class/form sheet from your Excel file

Example: F1J, F2W, F3J, F4W

📝 How to Use (Step 3-4)

Step 3: Upload Template

Choose your Word report card template (.docx file)

Tip: Use placeholders like {{NAME}}, {{MATH}}, {{COMMENTS}}

Step 4: Generate & Download

Click the Generate button and wait for processing

Download: All reports at once as ZIP or individually
